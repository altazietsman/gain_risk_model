#Data table paramaters

Date - daily date
Time - Time expressed in hours
solar - incoming solar irradiance (W m-2)
temp - air temperatyre (Celcius)
wind - wind speed (m s-1)
RH - relative humidity (%)
soil_pressure - soil water pressure (MPa)
P_atm - atmospheric pressure (kPa)
Tleaf - leaf temperature (Celcius)
b - vulnerability curve parameter
c - vulnerability curve parameter
Vmax25 - Maximum Carboxylation rate at 25 degree Celcius (umol s-1 m-2)
Jmax25 - Maximum electron transport rate at 25 degree Celsius (umol s-1 m-2)
r - CO2 compensation point rate at 25 degree Celcius (Pa)
XRI - Xylem recovery index
LABA - Leaf area to basal area ratio m2 m-2
